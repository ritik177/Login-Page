One of the top engineering schools in Uttar Pradesh, University Institute of Engineering & Technology (UIET) was established in 1996. It is a part of Chhatrapati Shahu Ji Maharaj University, Kanpur, and is situated in Kanpur. 

  • The institute aims to provide its students with a superior education through the design of the curriculum, the creation of new courses, and the use of a forward-thinking, creative teaching approach.
  • Over 1500 new students have enrolled at UIET, and there are over six different engineering specialties (Chemical, Computer Science and Information Technology, Mechanical Engineering, Electronics & Communication, and Material Science & Engineering).
  • It also provides other career-focused degrees including the MCA, M.Sc., and M.Phil. 
  • In Uttar Pradesh, UIET is ranked 30th out of 221 engineering colleges.
  • The Institute has total academic autonomy, and the curriculum is frequently updated to meet the sector's present and foreseeable demands.
  • The institute works hard to encourage entrepreneurship among the students and actively promotes innovation.
  •  The institute encourages its students to take part in cultural, sporting, literary, and social service programmes throughout their time here to ensure their holistic growth.

UIET Kanpur Admission Process

For undergraduate course admission at the Institute of Engineering and Technology, the AKTU Entrance Examination & Counseling are used. Candidates for postgraduate course admission must have graduated with a minimum GPA of 50%. For more information refer official website:

How to Apply?

  • Admission in BTech programs is through the AKTU Entrance exam process while BCA and other postgraduate programs are through University Entrance Examination.
  • Applicants are required to fill the application form online through the official website and pay the application fee online via credit/ debit card or net-banking and no other mode of payment is allowed.
  • Provide all the necessary details like academic details, personal details, and previous qualifications. Keep the printout of the confirmation page for record and are also advised to note down the application form number for any future reference.

UIET Kanpur Placements

According to prior performance, the UIET Kanpur placement cell has been quite good. Through campus interviews at the college, students were placed. Students who graduated from this college are employed by well-known and highly regarded MNCs. Students at this college receive training from the placement cell by participating in simulated aptitude tests, group discussions, and preparation for technical and HR interviews. Additionally, it arranges for students to be placed right away and senior business staff to give coaching lectures. In recent years, some of the top employers have included Wipro, HCL, Mindzone, Reliance Power, Infosys, Mahindra, IBM, and numerous others.

UIET Kanpur Scholarship

At the start of the semester, students apply for scholarships by contacting the college administration. The scholarship is offered by the Uttar Pradesh government, and applicants must apply online before contacting the college administration to continue the application process. By the start of the following semester, this award reimburses students for their costs in their bank accounts.

FAQs

Ques. What is the university's system for placing students?

Ans. A placement unit at the college arranges internships and summer training for its students. Their graduates are hired by both public and private organizations. They arrange campus interviews, offer career guidance, and help students get ready for interviews.

Ques. What standards apply for admission?

Ans. The student must have at least a 45% grade point average in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics in 10+2 from an accredited board and pass the entrance exam.

Ques. Are there any events that the university puts on?

Ans. Yes, the institution schedules events throughout the semester such the Farewell Function, Spandan, Techmart, Sportiva, and Fresher's Welcome. The Freshers Welcome, held at the start of each session for the newly enrolled students, kicks off the Institute's student activities. The first week of September is often when this event takes place.

Ques. Does the B.Voc in Fashion Technology and Interior Design have an entrance exam?

Ans. Yes, there is an entrance exam for B.Voc programmes in interior design and fashion technology. On the University of Kanpur's official website, you can register for it.

Ques. Is attendance required?

Ans. The minimum attendance requirement for each course throughout each semester is 75%. Medical justifications or any other genuine/unavoidable reason is accepted as justification for a 15% relaxation.
